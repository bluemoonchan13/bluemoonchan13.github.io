Cube

This project was created to rethink what a wall is and what surfaces are. It is about how we use walls and surfaces and just rethinking placement of where we sleep and a different correlation of gardening.

The challenges of this project was the conception and thought process of the project. The goal was to create that connection between gardening and sleeping without putting them in the same room. So I considered what really made a room and what a wall is and how connections can be made with them. I began with a simple cube and considered ways in modifying it and creating the most space out of that cube with as little materials as possible. So I used one and a 1/4 wall, one floor, and one ceiling.

My goal was the try to cut out all of the fluff and unnecessary things to emphasize what I would need while still trying to use mostly natural materials.
