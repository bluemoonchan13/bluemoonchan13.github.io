Hide Away

This project was made to be a closed away gallery without feeling closed off. I wanted this space to be able being surrounded by nature and away from the noise. So I filled the gallery with photos that I was able to capture delicate moments.

I wanted the viewer to be able to walk through the space and be immersed in what they saw and also be allowed to wander freely without being inclosed. So I placed viewports through two different sections of the gallery where you would not be able to directly see the other room horizontally.
