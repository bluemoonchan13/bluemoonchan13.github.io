Office Space

The office space was to be able to replicate light in an enclosed space. The room was created with different types of spot lights and lights embedded into the wall without over exposing anything in the room. I also needed to create a realistic setting in which the office is in while still making the space aesthetically pleasing. And showing my personality through the design.
