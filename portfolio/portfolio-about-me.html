About Me

My name is Gina Ng and I plan on having several pets. I want a dog, cat, snake, bunny, guinea pig, mini pig, and maybe a sea otter (it depends on if I can get a nice pool or not). I love animals and nice walks in my backyard. I game occasionally and have spurts of when I decide to read.

My thought process is odd and very jumpy but when I come up with an idea that I like I stick to it and try to bring it to reality. But I think my biggest flaw in my thought processes is that I need to have all of the information whether it seems irrelevant or not in order to begin to brainstorm ideas for a project. Because once I’m generally set on an idea, it’s very hard for me to change my way of thinking for that project.

But my general day to day thought process would go like: coding, language, I should probably learn chinese, I don’t visit my grandpa often, I need to change the oil in my car, I have to drive to sf next week, I need to go grocery shopping, I should go to Michael’s later, maybe my friend wants to come, did he clean his room yet?, I should have noodles for dinner. It makes sense in my head but when I type it out, it doesn’t seem to have a logical path but it does. Just trust me on it.
